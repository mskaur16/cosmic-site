ABOUT

ECLIPSE is a student-built initiative that aims to clarify concepts such as astrophysics, cosmology, planetary science, and rocketry using simple language and visual explanations. This website presents advanced subjects in a more understandable way so that more students can engage with and enjoy space science.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER

The founder of this website is a school-going student with a deep passion for space and the cosmos. While researching astrophysics topics, she often had to switch between multiple websites to fully understand one concept, which felt frustrating. That experience inspired her to create ECLIPSE — a platform where space-related topics can be explored clearly in one place, without confusion.
